Decentralized Storage Roadmap

Follow our step-by-step process to set up and access your private cloud.

1

Launch Desktop App

Run the MyCloud executable application on your laptop or computer. No code compilers or Python dependencies required—the background server engine initializes quietly in the background.

2

Connect Storage (First run only)

Plug in a USB pendrive or external drive. On first startup, the Setup Wizard helps link your storage path. Future runs skip this step automatically and mount your storage instantly.

4

Publish Secure Tunnel

Click "Publish Tunnel" inside your Admin Panel. Instantly generate a secure public link to access and stream your files from your phone or any network globally.

3

Lock Your Vault

Access your local dashboard to secure your cloud. Register your administrator credential profile and configure access permissions to keep files protected.
